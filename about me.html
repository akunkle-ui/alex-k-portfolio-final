about me

Alex Kunkle Photographer Designer Artist

I'm Alex K, a long-time artist and creative. As a military kid, I grew up all over the place! I have lived in Hawaii the longest, however, and feel happiest here. My interest in graphic design started a few years back during my time at Leeward CC. I love all things creative! Photography, painting, writing, graphic design, film, you name it! While I do mostly cosplay photography, I love doing all portrait work!
